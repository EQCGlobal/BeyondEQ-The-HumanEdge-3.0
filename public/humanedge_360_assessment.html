Self Assessment
Before you begin
You are about to rate yourself on 68 observable behaviors across 15 skills. This is the self-rater version. Be as honest as you can — the gap between your self-rating and your observers' ratings is the most important data point in the entire instrument.
Rate what you actually do — not what you intend to do
Intentions are invisible to others. Observable behavior is what the 360 measures. Rate yourself on what others would consistently see — not on your best moments or your aspirations.
